OUR DINING VENUES

Modern comfort & traditional coziness! We have furnished our guest rooms with modern yet timeless accents and hope that you feel at home in our house.

All guest rooms are furnished to a high standard (the furniture was made by a carpenter) and have a shower, toilet, hairdryer, and TV. Most have a balcony/terrace and include breakfast. These double rooms can also be booked as single rooms.

As a Salzburger Seenland Card inclusive company, you will also receive many discounts as our overnight guest, as well as free entry to outdoor pools, museums, and much more from May 1st to October 31st!
